Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about art commissions

How long does a commission take?

Most commissions take 2-4 weeks to complete, depending on the complexity and medium. Digital works are typically faster than traditional paintings.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, I require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon completion before delivery.

Can I request revisions?

Absolutely! The commission process includes two or three rounds of revisions during the process to keep you updated and to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final artwork.

Do you ship internationally?

No, I don't ship traditional artwork internationally. Digital commissions, on the other hand, can be sent online to clients worldwide.
