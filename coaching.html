Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about art coaching

Do I need to have prior art experience?

Not at all! I work with complete beginners to advanced artists. Each coaching plan is tailored to your current skill level and goals.

What art supplies do I need?

For beginners, we'll start with basic supplies and recommend additions as you progress. I'll provide a supply list before we begin.

How often should I schedule coaching sessions?

For optimal progress, weekly or fortnightly sessions are recommended, but we can adjust based on your schedule and goals. Consistency is key for artistic development.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! Gather 2-5 friends for a small group workshop and receive discounted rates per person. This is a great option for friends who want to learn together.
