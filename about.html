Meet Gabriel

Hi, I'm Gabriel, a professional artist originally from Ecuador, who spent the last five years living and creating in Tokyo, Japan. Recently, I relocated to Melbourne, where I continue my journey as a painter, illustrator, and art coach.

Art has always been at the center of my life. Over the years, I have worked across many mediums: digital painting, inks, watercolors, and acrylics. But I keep coming back to a style that blends bold storytelling with fine detail. My time in Tokyo deeply influenced my visual language, and now in Melbourne, I’m inspired by the city’s vibrant art scene and diverse culture.

Today, my work focuses on three main areas:

Art Commissions in Melbourne

Whether you’re looking for a personalized portrait, a unique gift, or a custom piece for your home or business, I create one-of-a-kind artworks tailored to your vision.

International Art Commissions

Distance is no barrier to creativity. I work with clients around the world, delivering high-quality digital paintings and illustrations that can be printed or shared online.

Art Coaching & Classes

I offer private art coaching in Melbourne, guiding students of all levels through drawing, painting, and creative exploration. My approach is hands-on and tailored, helping each student grow their artistic voice with confidence.

My Approach

I approach each project with meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to capturing the essence of my subjects. Whether it's a digital illustration, a traditional style artworks, or an art class, I bring the same level of passion and dedication.

My style blends contemporary techniques with traditional influences, resulting in artwork that feels both timeless and modern. I work closely with clients to understand their vision and bring it to life in a way that exceeds expectations.

If my style resonates with you, I’d love to connect. Whether it’s to bring your idea to life through a commission, or to support your artistic journey through coaching.
