International Commission FAQs

Common questions about international digital commissions

How do we handle time zone differences?

I'm flexible with scheduling consultations and progress updates to accommodate your time zone. We can communicate via email, messaging apps, or schedule video calls at mutually convenient times.

What payment methods do you accept internationally?

I accept PayPal, bank transfers, and other secure international payment methods.

Can I print the digital artwork locally?

Yes! All digital commissions are delivered in high resolution formats suitable for printing. I can provide recommendations for print sizes and materials if needed.

What file formats do you deliver?

I typically provide final artwork in multiple formats including high-resolution JPEG, PNG, PDF, and layered PSD files where appropriate. Files are delivered via secure cloud transfer services.
